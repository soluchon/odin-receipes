Cucumber Mint Citrus Water

This is a very healthy and infused water receipe. Delight yourself

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts water
  • 1 lemon sliced, or as desired
  • 2 limes, sliced, or as desired
  • 1/2 cup mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup sliced cucumber, or as desired

Preparation

  1. Pour water into a pitcher
  2. Mix lemon slices,lime slices, mint leaves and cucumber together in a bowl; add water and stir.
  3. Refrigerate water misture, stirring 1 time per day, until flavours have infused, 2 to 3 days

Note:

For kids, filter out all leaves, fruits and cucumber. Any variety of citrus fruits can be used in place of lemon and lime
